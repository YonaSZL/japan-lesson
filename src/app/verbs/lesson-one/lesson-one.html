Basics of Japanese Verbs

Five groups

  • Group 1: -う verbs
  • Group 2: -る verbs
  • Group 3: する verbs
  • Group 4: Irregulars verbs
  • Group 5: Adjectival verbs

Bases / stems

Part of the verb without the conjugation

  • Group 1: Depends on the sub group; ex : -む ->　ま　み　む　め　も
  • Group 2: Never changes; ex : 食べ
  • Group 3: Noun + する; Takes する's stem; ex : 勉強し
  • Group 4: No pattern ; Memorize them; ex : し
  • Group 5: Never changes; Noun or Noun + し for emotions; ex : 可愛 or 楽し
